About Me

I'm Aanchal Baid, an aspiring Interior Designer currently pursuing my studies at Dezyne Ecole College. Passionate about transforming spaces, I specialize in creating thoughtful, functional, and aesthetic environments that reflect individuality and purpose.
With a strong interest in spatial planning, design theory, and material selection, I’m constantly exploring new trends and tools to sharpen my creative and technical skills. I'm especially enthusiastic about building a career in the interior design industry, with a focus on developing compelling design folios and concept-driven interiors.

I'm actively working on expanding my portfolio through academic projects and freelance opportunities, and I'm eager to contribute to innovative, people-centered design solutions. Let’s connect and create inspiring spaces together.
